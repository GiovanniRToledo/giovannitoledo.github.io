This application allows users to take on the role of either a tourist or a tour guide . Tour guides are on a location based selection and are expected to have working knowledge of the location. The amount charged per tour per hour is based on the discretion of the tour guide. Tourists can view a selection of tour guides based on their star rating, amount of reviews, and a selection of services that are offered. Tourists can filter by travel dates and language. This application makes it possible for those wanting to explore new cities by finding a plethora of key information pertaining to the “verified local” tour guides in their chosen city, including photos, reviews, ratings and pricing. Aside from professional tour guides where services are formal and straight to the point, users are likely to find affordable prices.
